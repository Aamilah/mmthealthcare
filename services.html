Services

Community Access

This service helps clients engage with their local community by participating in various groups and activities. The goal is to build a support network, foster social connections, and encourage the exploration of new hobbies and interests.

Joining Community Groups

Assisting clients in connecting with local groups focused on arts and crafts, gardening, book clubs, and more.

Attending Events and Classes:

Helping clients find and participate in community events, classes, and other social activities.

Building Friendships

Encouraging the development of lasting and supportive friendships through regular engagement in community activities.

Exploring New Interests

Supporting clients in trying out new hobbies and activities to broaden their social and recreational horizons​.

Services

Domestic Tasks and Personal Care

This service is aimed at ensuring that clients' homes are safe, clean, and comfortable. It involves assistance with various household tasks and personal care needs to help clients maintain a good quality of life and independence.

Cleaning Tasks

Regular cleaning schedules and task planning, including vacuuming, dusting, and organizing.

Seasonal Cleaning

Specific tasks such as carpet cleaning, window cleaning, and spring cleaning.

Home Maintenance

Assistance with shopping for and replacing household items like furniture, bedding, and curtains.

Personal Care

Support with daily routines such as bathing, dressing, and grooming to ensure personal hygiene and well-being​.

Services

Budgeting and Shopping

This service helps clients manage their finances and plan their shopping needs, both for daily necessities and long-term purchases. The aim is to ensure clients can live comfortably within their means while obtaining what they need and want.

Daily Shopping Needs

Planning and budgeting for groceries, clothing, and entertainment (e.g., TV subscriptions).

Long-Term Purchases

Assistance with budgeting and purchasing items like furniture, electronics, and household appliances.

Helping clients find the best deals and discounts to maximize their budget.

Meal Planning

Support in creating meal plans and managing a food budget, including access to cooking classes if needed

Services

Sport and Leisure

This service is focused on facilitating clients' participation in sports and recreational activities that they enjoy. It aims to promote physical health, mental well-being, and social interaction through various leisure activities.

Identifying Interests

Discussing clients' hobbies and interests to understand what they enjoy and exploring potential new interests.

Planning Day Trips

Organizing trips to cultural attractions (museums, theatres, art galleries), theme parks, zoos, and national parks.

Sporting Events

Arranging visits to sporting attractions, including match days and stadium tours.

Recreational Activities

Facilitating participation in activities such as hiking, gardening, and joining sports teams or clubs​
